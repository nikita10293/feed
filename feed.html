Feed Our Heros


The only true heroes during the coronavirus crisis are our ‘Knights in Shining PPE kits.



Our Doctors and Nurses have served their oath with unflinching loyalty and commitment. These heroes have stood tall and have tirelessly been there for each one of us every single day and night throughout this crisis.

We at GeneBandhu believe that the least we can do for these brave warriors is to show our appreciation for their monumental and selfless efforts.

Along with The Big Chill Cafe and Aditi Chaudhary, we proudly present our campaign ‘Feed Our heroes’, to provide hot and delicious free meals to our front-line workers.

What had started as a small personal project has been so appreciated that we would like to continue this as long as the hospitals are overcrowded. Our meals provide them with a little joy and relief in this very difficult time.

To fulfill this goal, we require approximately Rs. 18,000 - 20,000 per day, depending on the menu. Any donations towards this effort will be appreciated.

We hope our meals help bring a smile to our warriors’ faces!


* Kindly note that All donations are eligible for IT deduction u/s 80G. Please provide us with your details (name, address, PAN number) along with the amount donated via email (info@genebandhu.in). This will enable us to generate 80G certificates.
