Donate Oxygen


What oxygen is to the lungs, such is hope to the meaning of life.



With the second wave of the corona pandemic came an unexpected and sudden shortage of medical grade oxygen. To combat this situation and help out patients in need of oxygen, GeneBandhu has partnered up with Kashmiri Pandit Blood Bank (KPBB) / Kashmiri Pandit Corona Fighters (KPCF) to initiate Project Oxygen.

Project Oxygen started with the humble aim of having 15 oxygen concentrators in our arsenal and covered the Delhi-NCR area. With great support and appreciation, we have scaled up our initiative and now aim to have upto 100 concentrators. The project has also spread in various cities across Northern India in the states of Punjab, Rajasthan and Jammu & Kashmir.

Our courageous volunteers are actively involved in supplying these life saving machines to Covid patients at their homes. Any donations towards this effort will be appreciated.
